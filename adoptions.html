Adopting a Pet from Saving Paws Animal Rescue

Thank you for considering saving the life of a homeless pet. Adopting your next furry family member is a very rewarding experience and they are very special companions.

When you adopt one of our pets, you save more lives. By taking home a rescued pet and making it part of your family, you open up a space for another pet to be saved. Your adoption is a gift that keeps on giving.

We have a wide variety of pets waiting to find their new people — different sizes and colors, breeds and breed mixes, and different personalities.

How to meet our adoptable pets

We currently do not have regular business hours.  By making an appointment you’ll be able to meet the pet you are interested in. To adopt from Saving Paws, just follow these simple steps:

  • Fill out an adoption application online at www.savingpaws.com.

(Processing your application can take anywhere from 1-4 days depending on response time by references.  Once you are approved, you may return to pick up your new companion(s). All applications are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. If your application is not filled out in full it will not be processed.)

  • Set an appointment to meet an adoptable pet. We are fully volunteer run and don’t have regular hours, so we will set up a time that works for you to come visit. We are located in north Appleton and when you set up an appointment we will give you directions to our rescue.
